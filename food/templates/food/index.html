Welcome! 101 Cookbooks is a California food blog by Heidi Swanson, focused on healthy recipes for everyday. It features over 700 vegetarian recipes, whole foods recipes, and vegan recipes, plus the occasional sweet treat.
I’ve also written four cookbooks, and am a New York Times Best Selling author. Below you’ll see some of my favorite recipes. There are lots of vegan recipes, vegetarian recipes, Instant Pot recipes, and WFPB recipes. Please enjoy!
